Shine the Spotlight on our Joint Achievements

Polycom’s Partner Awards recognize top regional and global partners for outstanding achievements in sales growth, services, innovation and more. Honorees are integral contributors within the Polycom Partner Network, a worldwide ecosystem of alliance and channel partners.

Gala Awards Dinner

Wednesday, February 12, 2014

Special evening filled with celebration and recognition for outstanding achievements in 2013. Come celebrate with this year’s winners.

Theater and Country Awards Luncheon

Thursday, February 13, 2014

Luncheon to recognize outstanding business partners in each theater for their achievements, innovation, customer-focus and competency.
