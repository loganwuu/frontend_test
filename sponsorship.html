The TEAM Polycom 2014 Sponsorship Opportunity

Promote, connect and drive your Polycom business by sponsoring TEAM Polycom EXPO.

Create new sales opportunities, generate leads and position your company as a premier Polycom ecosystem partner.

Each sponsorship level offers a variety of benefits that include branding, networking and the ability to demonstrate solutions on the EXPO show floor. In 2014, sponsorship offerings have been expanded to offer more opportunities and more visibility for your organization!
