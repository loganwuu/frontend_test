Winning in 2014! – Your Competitive Advantage

Ed Allison, Senior Director, Competitive Solutions

As the Unified Communications and Collaboration (UC&C) market continues its rapid evolution, customers can be overwhelmed with the number of options available, delaying their purchase decisions. The market is crowded with competition, including traditional competitors such as Cisco, Vidyo, Avaya, and LifeSize, as well as a wave of new entrants to the market such as Blue Jeans, Acano, and Pexip – so many companies, technologies, delivery options, and business models that it can be confusing for prospects and existing customers. This session will explain how Polycom navigates these perplexing times to enable partners, service providers, and customers to win. You’ll get specifics on the competition’s strategies and see live on-stage demonstrations to better understand the Polycom difference to win business.
